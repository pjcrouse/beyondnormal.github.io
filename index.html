Beyond Normal – Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Overview

Beyond Normal respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. All information, including your training maxes, workouts, and medals, is stored locally on your device. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.

Data Collection and Storage

The app does not collect personal information, health data, or usage data from you or your device. Any data you enter or generate while using Beyond Normal remains on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

Purchases and Subscriptions

All in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. Beyond Normal does not have access to your payment information or Apple ID details.

Third-Party Services

Beyond Normal does not integrate with or share data with any external services, analytics providers, or advertisers.

Your Consent

By using Beyond Normal, you consent to this privacy policy. If you disagree with it, please discontinue use of the app.
