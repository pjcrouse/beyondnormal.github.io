Overview

Beyond Normal is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your workout data is yours alone. This policy explains how we handle data and protect your privacy.

Privacy Promise

  • ✓ All data stored locally on your device
  • ✓ No user accounts or registration required
  • ✓ No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • ✓ No data collection, selling, or sharing
  • ✓ You control when data leaves your device

Data Storage

All data you create in Beyond Normal is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Workout history: All logged workouts, sets, reps, and weights
  • Personal records: Your PR tracking and achievements
  • Custom exercises: Assistance exercises and custom movements
  • Program settings: Your training maxes, cycle configuration, and preferences
  • Notes: Any notes or comments you add to workouts

Important: This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it. There is no cloud backup, no server synchronization, and no automatic uploads.

Data Control & Deletion

You have complete control over your data at all times:

Export Your Data

Navigate to Data Management in the app to export your data in JSON or CSV format. Exported files are yours to keep, analyze, or backup as you see fit.

Delete Your Data

Individual Items

Delete specific workouts or personal records directly from Data Management. Deletions are immediate and permanent.

Complete Reset

Use Delete All Data in Data Management to clear everything instantly. This action cannot be undone.

Uninstall App

Removing Beyond Normal from your device automatically deletes all locally stored training data.

Purchases & Subscriptions

Beyond Normal offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple:

  • Payment processing: Handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store
  • Purchase validation: We receive only a non-personal transaction receipt from Apple to verify your purchase and unlock features
  • No payment details: We never see, store, or have access to your credit card, billing address, or payment information
  • Subscription management: Managed through your Apple ID settings, not by us

Apple's privacy policy governs their handling of payment data. We recommend reviewing it at apple.com/legal/privacy.

What We Don't Collect

Beyond Normal is built to respect your privacy. We explicitly do not collect:

  • No personal identifiable information (name, email, phone, address)
  • No analytics or usage statistics
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking cookies
  • No location data or device identifiers
  • No crash reports or diagnostic data
  • No behavioral tracking or user profiling
  • No third-party analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, etc.)

Communication

The only time we may receive personal information is if you contact us directly via email for support. In such cases:

  • We'll only use your email address to respond to your inquiry
  • We won't add you to mailing lists without explicit consent
  • We won't share your contact information with third parties
  • Support emails are deleted after your issue is resolved

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data security relies on your device's built-in protections:

  • iOS device encryption and Face ID/Touch ID protections
  • Your device passcode and security settings
  • iOS sandbox protections that isolate app data

We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest iOS security patches and using a strong passcode.

Children's Privacy

Beyond Normal does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require accounts or personal information, making it suitable for all ages. Parents should supervise device access as appropriate.

Changes to This Policy

As Beyond Normal evolves, we may update this privacy policy. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
