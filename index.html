Adaptive Autoregulation

Training maxes that respond to reality.

Classic 5/3/1 adds 5 or 10 lbs every cycle regardless of how you're recovering. Beyond Normal watches your AMRAPs and your readiness — and adjusts each lift independently.

  • Hold what's fatigued. Lifts the model flags as under-recovered repeat at current TM.
  • Progress what's strong. Lifts with momentum and clean readiness keep climbing.
  • You decide. Every suggestion is a consent card — accept, override, or progress normally.
