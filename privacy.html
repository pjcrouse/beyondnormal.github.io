Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Beyond Normal rejects “normal” data practices. No accounts. No tracking. No dark patterns. Your training lives on your device — under your control.

Data on Your Device

Your workouts, PRs, notes, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone. They never leave your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Data Control & Deletion

  • Export: In the app, open Data Management to export JSON or CSV for your records or analysis.
  • Granular deletion: Delete individual workouts or PRs directly in Data Management.
  • Delete all: Use Delete All Data in Data Management to clear everything instantly.
  • Uninstall: Removing the app also erases all locally stored training data.

Purchases

Subscriptions and lifetime access are processed by Apple. We receive a non-personal validation receipt to confirm your purchase and unlock features. We never see your payment details.

We Do Not Collect

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No personal information unless you contact us directly (for example, by email).
