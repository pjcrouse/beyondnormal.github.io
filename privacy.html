Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Beyond Normal • Strength is designed to store your training locally on your device. We keep things simple: we don’t run ads, we don’t use third-party analytics SDKs, and we don’t sell personal data.

What we collect

  • Local training data. Your workouts, PRs, notes, and settings are saved on your device.
  • Purchases. App Store purchases (subscription or lifetime) are validated by Apple. We receive non-personal purchase receipts from Apple to unlock features.

What we don’t collect

  • No third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No personal contact information unless you email us voluntarily.

Data export & deletion

You can export your data from within the app. To delete your data, remove the app from your device; this deletes locally stored training logs.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@beyondnormal.app.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We’ll update the effective date above and publish changes on this page.
