Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Beyond Normal • Strength respects your privacy and your ownership of your data. The app is designed to work entirely on your device — no accounts, no tracking, no cloud sync.

Data on Your Device

Your workouts, PRs, notes, and settings are stored locally and never leave your device unless you choose to export them. You have complete control — you can export, back up, or delete everything directly from within the app.

Purchases

All purchases (subscription or lifetime access) are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We receive only a non-personal validation receipt from Apple to confirm your purchase and unlock features. We never see your payment details or personal information.

We Do Not Collect

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No personal information unless you contact us directly (for example, by email).

Data Removal

To delete all app data, simply remove the app from your device. This will permanently erase all locally stored training information.

Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at support@beyondnormal.app.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Updates will be published here with a new effective date.
